The Main Characters Of Just Roll With It ; Riptide!

Chip

19 Year Old Human Rogue

Chip's dream is to be a great pirate just like his father figure and idol, Arlon James.

Fun fact: Chip was found by Arlon as an orphan who claimed to want to be the strongest on the chip while attempting to say ship. This is how he got his name.

Gillion Tidestrider

22 Year Old Triton Paladin

Gillion Tidestrider emerged from the undersea and dedicated his life to fufill his destiny.

Fun fact: Gillion was born into a prophecy where he is destined to choose whether the land or sea remains.

Jay Ferin

21 Year Old Human Ranger

Jay Ferin left the navy after the death of her sister to find out what happened.

Fun fact: The Ferin family are almost always high ranking Navy. Jay is an undercover spy.
